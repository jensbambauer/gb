Print

Our prints (technically they’re digital photo exposures) are made with highest grade Fuji Color DPII FG Crystal Archive paper, allowing precise color reproduction and maximum longevity.

You have the option of glossy or matte, the latter is recommended if you plan to frame the piece. While glossy paper (which is used for our Dibond® process) carries a bit more “punch“, matte paper effectively minimizes reflections when framed under glass.

Utilizing a Durst Lambda, the industry leading photochemical printer, reference image quality is maintained during the process of digital laser exposure and traditional chemical development.

A white border of 1cm / 0.4“ around the print is added to allow easy framing. Every piece comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.

Aluminum Dibond®

Our pieces are available in the sophisticated, frameless Aluminum Dibond® production technique, a modern, visually minimalist option to framing.

The photo exposure is sandwiched (using clear, permanently elastic silicone) between 4mm highest grade acrylic glass and 3mm of Dibond®, a rigid aluminum / polyethylene composite material. A hidden aluminum rail on the back adds stability and allows effortless mounting.

Milled with CNC precision, the finished piece weighs 5 kg / 11 lbs and appears to be floating on the wall. Every piece comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.

Frame

Our frames are hand built and represent an addition to the Aluminum Dibond® production technique, allowing a mat-less frame with the piece extending all the way to the edges.

The photo exposure is sandwiched (using clear, permanently elastic silicone) between 2mm highest grade acrylic glass and 3mm of Dibond®, a rigid aluminum / polyethylene composite material. A hidden aluminum rail on the back adds stability and allows effortless mounting.

The additional wooden frame is available in three colors: Black (Oak), White (Maple) and unpainted Maple. Now it’s up to you to choose a combination that benefits the piece and suits your space.

The framed piece weighs 6 kg / 13.2 lbs and comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.
