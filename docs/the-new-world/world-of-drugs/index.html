Print

Our Lambda C-Prints are made with highest grade Fuji Color DPII FG Crystal Archive paper, allowing precise color reproduction and maximum longevity.

You have the option of glossy or matte, the latter is recommended if you plan to have the piece framed by a shop of your choice. While glossy paper (which is used for our Aluminum Dibond® process) enables a slightly higher contrast, matte paper effectively minimizes reflections when framed under glass.

Utilizing a Durst Lambda, the industry-leading photochemical printer, reference image quality is maintained during the process of digital laser exposure and traditional chemical development.

A white border of 1cm / 0.4“ around the print is added to allow easy framing. Every piece comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.

Aluminum Dibond®

Our pieces are available in the sophisticated, frameless Aluminum Dibond® production technique, a modern, visually minimalist option to framing.

The photo exposure is sandwiched (using clear, permanently elastic silicone) between 4mm high grade acrylic glass and 3mm of Dibond®, a rigid aluminum / polyethylene composite material. A hidden aluminum rail on the back adds stability and allows effortless mounting.

Milled with CNC precision, the finished piece weighs 7 kg / 15.4 lbs and will appear to be floating on the wall once mounted. Every piece comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.

Frame

Our frames are hand built and function as an addition to the Aluminum Dibond® production technique, allowing a mat-less frame with the piece extending all the way to the edges.

The photo exposure is sandwiched (using clear, permanently elastic silicone) between 2mm high grade acrylic glass and 3mm of Dibond®, a rigid aluminum / polyethylene composite material. A hidden aluminum rail on the back adds stability and allows effortless mounting.

The added wooden frame measures 2cm / 0.8“ in width and 3cm / 1.2“ in depth. There are three available options: black (oak wood), white (maple wood) and maple. You’re free to choose a combination that benefits the piece and suits your space.

The framed piece weighs 6 kg / 13.2 lbs and comes with a signed certificate of authenticity carrying the serial number.
